PROJECT #2


For my green screen project, I decided to do my own take of the POV videos. This POV is based on the viewer being a girl sitting alone at a Mexican dance (a Baile). Suddenly another girl who is also sitting out the Nortenas sparks a conversation. It relates to my personal experiences since I'm not a fan of this particular style of dance. You are then taken out to dance once El Payaso de Rodeo (a popular line dance) comes on (this one is my personal favorite). I wanted to use a party based background for the green screen since it's common to have strobe lights and a dark atmosphere at these events. The songs I used are Te Vas a Acordar de Mi by La Fiera de Ojinaga and Payaso de Rodeo by Caballo Dorado. This was really fun to do since it blends my personal experiences and love for acting/art into one.
